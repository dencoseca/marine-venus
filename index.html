Get to know Marine Venus

Marine Venus has four double births each with their own en-suite, plus two fore crew cabins equiped with their own bathroom facilities. Being a catamaran, Marine Venus offers ample space inside and on deck for all aboard and a smoother ride and extra stability due to its twin hull design.

Relax inside in the fully air-conditioned galley, or have dinner on the deck and enjoy a rum while being surrounded by a multicolored sky (it is the caribbean after all).
